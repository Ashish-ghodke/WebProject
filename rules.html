Rules and Regulations

  • Wear Proper Attire: Ensure you are dressed in the appropriate karate uniform (gi) during training sessions.
  • Maintain Cleanliness: Keep the dojo clean and tidy. Respect the training space.
  • Practice Safety: Always prioritize safety during practice. Use protective gear when necessary.

  • No Distractions: Turn off phones and other distractions during training sessions.
  • Commit to Training: Attend classes regularly and practice at home to improve your skills.
  • Have Fun: Enjoy the process of learning and growing in your martial arts journey!

Belt Exam Rules and System

We conduct belt exams every 3 months, overseen by external coaches or masters who hold significant positions in karate. There are 9 belts in karate, following this ranking:

White

Yellow

Orange

Green

Blue

Purple

Brown 3rd

Brown 2nd

Brown 1st

Black Belt

If you pass the exam, you will receive your belt and certificate on the same day. However, if you fail, you must retake the exam with a maximum of 3 attempts.
